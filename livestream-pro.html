Livestream Pro

Designing and launching a new live streaming product for enterprises

The companyâ€™s existing live streaming product was cumbersome to use due to poor design and too many features that had been built one-by-one on top of each other, without too much thought. The product also lacked in technical capabilities when it came to streaming and producing events.

The decision was therefore made to launch a new live streaming product that would be provide state of the art technical solutions for producing events and that would enable event managers to set up events in a matter of minutes.

The development of the new product had already started as I joined the design team. With a design team of only two designers, me and my colleague worked closely together on all UX and UI related topics.

Goal

To create a live streaming product for enterprises that performs better than the existing product .

Scope and timetable of the project

The timetable for the project was tight as a MVP of the website needed to be ready and live for an online event that was held four weeks after the project properly started. The project included creating the information architecture and the wireframes for the website. The user research had already been done.

My role & the team

Team: Jia Yin Loon (designer), Noorida Mohd Ismail (designer), Katri Eliander (designer), Sara Simoncini (UI), Nneka Edozien (developer). Throughout the project we also worked closely with the marketing department.

I and the two designers worked from the start together to comprehend the user persona, user journey and IA. The wireframe designs we divided amongst us but we collaborated closely throughout the whole process.
