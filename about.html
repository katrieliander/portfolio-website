Curious, empathic & goal-oriented describe me both as a designer and as a person.

I'm from Finland but my desire for new adventures brought me to Berlin 6 years ago. My curiosity has taken me all around the world (so far I have lived in 5 countries) and it also lead me to make my career transition into product design 4 years ago.

Since I was little I have been interested in human behavior and in understanding why people act the way they do. My mum says that I didn't stop asking "why" when I was a child. In my previous roles in SEO and digital marketing, I had several touch points with usability topics and my marketing background only makes me a stronger designer.

I approach design with a holistic approach and always think about optimizing user experiences end-to-end. I enjoy digging into data and analytics as much as I do getting creative and sketching ideas. I love how as a UX designer I can combine both creative and analytical work.

My first coffee in the morning, good food, sunsets, long walks, yoga, exploring new places and conversations with friends are things that bring me joy in my everyday life.
