I'm originally from Finland but my desire for new adventures brought me to Berlin 3 years ago. It's also in Berlin where I dared to make my career switch into UX Design.

I believe that all my previous work experiences were meant to lead me into a career in UX Design. Since I was little I have been interested in human behavior and in understanding why people act the way they do. My curious nature has taken me all around the world and so far I have lived in 5 countries.

I studied Economics and Marketing, whereafter I worked +5 years in SEO, mainly in the e-commerce field. Hence, even though I only quite recently transitioned into UX Design, I claim that I have already 5 years of experience in enhancing user experiences. SEO is about driving traffic to a website but after all, it's all about providing a good user experience.

As a SEO specialist, I have been optimizing landing pages to provide relevant search results for users' search queries, reduced image sizes to speed up websites and made sure HTML elements are semantically correct - all of which are equally relevant and important for UX Design. Together these two help shape digital products that bring value for both the user and the business!

My marketing background provides me thus with a more holistic understanding of growing products and increasing user experiences. I'm passionate about making products usable and attractive.
