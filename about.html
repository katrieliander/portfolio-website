Curious, empathic & goal-driven describe me both as a designer and as a person.

A little bit about me

I'm originally from Finland but my desire for new adventures brought me to Berlin 3 years ago. My curiosity has taken me all around the world and so far I have lived in 5 countries. It's finally in Berlin where I dared to make my career transition into UX Design.

My first coffee in the morning, sunsets, long walks, yoga, exploring new places and conversations with friends are things that bring me joy in my everyday life.

Why UX Design?

Since I was little I have been interested in human behavior and in understanding why people act the way they do. My mum says that I didn't stop asking "why" when I was a child.

In my previous jobs I have had several touch points with UX Design and with Designers, and I've always felt drawn to issues related to usability. So when I started learning more about the field, I immediately felt like it was the right match for me. I love how UX Design perfectly combines creative and analytical work.

How does my background strengthen me as a UX Designer?

I studied Economics and Marketing, whereafter I worked +5 years in SEO, mainly in the e-commerce field. SEO is about driving traffic to a website but after all, it's all about providing a good user experience.

As a SEO specialist, I have been optimizing landing pages to provide relevant search results for users' search queries, reduced image sizes to speed up websites and made sure HTML elements are semantically correct - all of which are equally relevant and important for UX Design. Together these two practices help shape digital products that bring value for both the user and the business.

What I can bring

My marketing background provides me a holistic understanding for growing products and increasing user experiences through design. I have an analytical mindset that loves to be challenged and tackle problems.

As an eager learner, I never want to stop developing myself and being curious. I'm not afraid to get out of my comfort zone - something that is proven by the leap I took when I decided to switch careers. I'm passionate about making products usable and attractive.
